Copyrighting

Copyrighting is a skill that requires extensive knowledge, and observations of human behavior. I believe that in order to write strong copy, it is important to understand the target audience as well as convince them why a particular product/service is necessary for them to go from their current state to their desired state.
Additionally, I believe that establishing a good relationship with the audience is valuable as there needs to be a level of trust established with you and your audience. With that being said, I write my copy with the intentions of gaining the audience's trust by ensuring them that by interacting with you, there will be an equal exchange in value. This will not only establish trust with you and your audience, but it will also motivate your audience to choose YOU over the competition.
My process of Copywriting includes robust methodologies used by the most successful copywriters which lead to incredible results. I will ask you the right questions, do the necessary research on all stakeholders involved, and I will overall make sure to meet YOUR particular needs at all times.
